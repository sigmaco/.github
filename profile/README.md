# SIGMA (Special Interest Group Management Administration)

SIGMA, acronym for *Special Interest Group Management Administration*, is a technocratic coalition established in 2011 by cybernetics enthusiasts from reunited self-defenses from Leste and Guanabara. Founded to cultivate a pragmatic culture of decision-making, strategic development, and long-term civilizational planning, SIGMA has since evolved into a distributed federation of specialized institutions, projects, and operational sectors coordinated through cybernetic principles and federal governance.

The SIGMA exists to design, develop, and deploy adaptive systems capable of addressing challenges that conventional institutions have repeatedly proven unable or unwilling to solve. Through systems science, cybernetics, strategic design, technological development, and knowledge preservation, SIGMA seeks to advance resilient, autonomous, and future-oriented alternatives to bureaucratic stagnation, technological dependency, and inherited structural limitations.

SIGMA FEDERATION is the federal technocratic administration, and the global and supranational expression, of SIGMA.

Everything happening here is bureaucratic by nature. Be used to it.

### Organism

SIGMA operates as a federation of specialized entities and initiatives.

Some of them will be involved with this channel, including but not limited to:

1. Estado-Major Pro Securidad, the top-level structure responsible for offensive and defensive security.
2. SIGMA Technology Group, responsible for research, engineering, software development, and technological operations.
3. Scitech, responsible for public-facing scientific and technological initiatives.
4. Engitech, responsible for specialized engineering and strategic development projects.
5. Archivo Federal, responsible for preservation, documentation, and historical continuity.
6. Bibliotheca Federal, responsible for knowledge organization, publication, and information stewardship.
7. Procuradoria Federal, responsible for legal assessment and advocacy of SIGMA.
8. Syndicance Federal, responsible for self-preservation of SIGMA against harmful personnel and internal affairs.

Together, these entities form a modular and transdisciplinary ecosystem capable of coordinating activities across multiple domains while maintaining operational flexibility.

### Public service and preservation

The SIGMA promotes technological competence, infrastructure independence, and the preservation of critical knowledge.

The SIGMA supports initiatives related to software preservation, digital archaeology, retrocomputing, and the maintenance of legacy systems. Over the years, SIGMA has assisted modding communities, software archivists, and technical preservationists in locating, documenting, and maintaining software critical to the continued operation of historical and discontinued technologies. Also, when available, providing juridic assistance to them.

Such efforts are undertaken in accordance with applicable legal frameworks and with consideration for the legitimate interests of rights holders and institutional partners.

SIGMA is sustained by contributors, researchers, engineers, archivists, designers, developers, and citizens committed to the construction of capable systems and durable institutions.

Contributions may take the form of research, development, preservation, documentation, education, infrastructure, or operational support. Individuals seeking assistance, collaboration, or participation may engage through any available SIGMA entry point.
